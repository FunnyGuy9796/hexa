Hexa is a custom 16-bit CPU architecture
that aims to be efficient and versatile.

Available Interrupts:
- Timer
- Keyboard
- Serial Port (BIOS Interrupt)
- Disk (BIOS Interrupt)

Exceptions:
- Invalid Opcode
- Invalid Operand
- Unaligned Access
- General Protection
- Illegal Instruction
- Divide by Zero
- Stack Overflow/Underflow
- Breakpoint
